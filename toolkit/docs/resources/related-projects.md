# Related Projects

Explore other projects on youth engagement in communities around the world.

## Youth Engagement Projects

**[The Reinventor Collective](https://www.reinventorcollective.org/)** - Brings together young people within Teach For America to co-create, amplify youth voices, and collaborate with adults in designing educational experiences.

**[By/With/For Youth](https://bywithforyouth.org/)** - A research and development initiative that partners with public media organizations to engage tweens and teens as co‑creators of content and media experiences.

**[Jugend entscheidet](https://www.jugend-entscheidet.de/)** - Empowers young people to choose and vote on real municipal decisions, giving them a direct voice in local governance.

**[Partnership for Young London](https://www.partnershipforyounglondon.org.uk/)** - Brings together youth voices, policymakers, and organizations to co-create solutions that improve the lives of young Londoners.

**[Jugend checkt Düsseldorf](https://jugend-checkt.de/)** - Involves young people in reviewing and assessing city policies to ensure they reflect youth needs and perspectives.

**[IDEATHON – European Association for Local Democracy](https://www.alda-europe.eu/)** - Engages young people in creative, cross-border problem-solving sessions to co-design ideas for better local democratic participation.

**[BeeWell Greater Manchester](https://www.beewellprogramme.org/)** - Measures and responds to young people's wellbeing through youth-led research to shape education, health, and community policies.
