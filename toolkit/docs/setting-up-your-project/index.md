# Setting up your own 'Co-Creating Our City' project

To set up your 'Co-Creating Our City' project for success, it is important to establish clear goals, a good team, strong community engagement, and buy-in from city administration and local partners who work with young people in your community. This chapter gives practical tips on setting up your own 'Co-Creating Our City' project, including how to build your project team and relationships with local partners and how to plan, fund, and budget for your project before you start.
